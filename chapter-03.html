The Core Argument

Financial expenditure is meaningless without an effective communication architecture.

You can spend millions building a school, but if the community doesn't understand it, wasn't consulted on it, or doesn't trust you, the project fails.

The true impact of CSR is determined by HOW it is communicated, not just by the money spent.

Constructed to map the lifecycle of a CSR initiative

The originating body providing funds and mandate.

2

Implementation Agency

Direct execution or NGO partnership on the ground.
