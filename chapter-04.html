The Five-Pillar Framework

Constructed to map the lifecycle of a CSR initiative

1

Corporate Entity

The originating body providing funds and mandate.

2

Implementation Agency

Direct execution or NGO partnership on the ground.

3

Initiative Type

The sectoral focus: Education, Health, Environment, Livelihood.

4

Impact Measurement

Assessing the actual betterment created (SROI, Pre/Post).

5

Communication Strategy

How the impact is conveyed back to stakeholders. (The focal variable of this study)

Pillars 1 & 2 Breakdown

P1: Corporate Entity

  • Scale: Large-cap vs Mid-cap vs Small-cap
  • Sector: Banking, Metal, Energy, Cement
  • Ownership: PSU vs Private Sector

P2: Implementation

  • Direct: Company's own CSR foundation
  • Partnered: Implementation via local NGOs
  • Exclusion: Government agencies (delimited out of scope)
